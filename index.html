we are "les Oreilles Bleues", a pop-rock band based in Munich, Germany.

You might wonder why the french name... well, there's a story behind it. It has to do with foreign languages and misunderstandings... it's a pretty funny story. If you ask we can tell it to you over a beer after a show.

We started playing together in February 2012. In June we borrowed a 16-track audio interface from a friend, filled our practice room with cables and microphones, and we recorded our first demo. It's only a first step, but we're always happy to hear what people think of our music. If you like it (or even if you don't), let us know!
