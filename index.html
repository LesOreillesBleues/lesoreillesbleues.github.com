An international band with international ambitions, "les Oreilles Bleues" is the result of an encounter between a swiss, a russian and a german who only had three things in common: a passion for music, a sense of adventure and... well, actually, that's it.

Florian's songwriting, with its nostalgic lyrics and crafted melodies, turns into a surprisingly powerful pop-rock when projected by Dmitry's energetic drums and Daniel's fuzzy bass.
